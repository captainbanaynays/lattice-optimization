where:

  • BiB_iBi​ is the i-th bin

Let's say that we have a specific space group that we want to bias towards. We can then use something like this:

Let ppp and qqq be the lower and upper bounds of the desired range to bias towards (so the mean μ\muμ of the distribution is centered between them). Define a new inverse distribution Ψ\PsiΨ:

where aaa, iii, and nnn mean the same the same thing, and zzz this time refers to how far apart ppp and qqq are on the distribution (when z=1z = 1z=1, ppp and qqq are 1 standard deviation apart).
